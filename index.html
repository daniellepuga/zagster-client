Zagster - Number of Rides vs Time of Year

This chart displays the relationship between month of year of 2017 versus number of times Zagster bikes were rented at OSU Cascades.

Total Rides

ABOUT: Using the Herokuapp website, we imported data from a Zagster database and utilized the Chart.js library to output the data in a visually appealing format. Data was taken from the website in an array format, converted to the correct format, and appropriately labeled & added to our chart. Labels were added in our chart to improve readability and make a clear division in months for all of 2017.
